If I was doing a website about a historical novel, and wanted to give a sense of older and more traditional typography, but still give it a feeling that isn't too formal, I'd change the typeface to something like Taviraj. The slightly rough serif font gives that old typeset feeling, but the large and rounded lowercase letters are more friendly and approachable, less authoritative. If I'd had more time, I might have moved towards more of a sepia-toned color scheme rather than black and white. For this example, however, I also wanted to show how you could modify the the type examples to show a light text on dark background approach.
